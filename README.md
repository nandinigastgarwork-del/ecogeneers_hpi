Water Quality Risk Assessment using HPI Index with Geo tagging 

A Streamlit-based interactive dashboard for assessing water quality by calculating the Heavy Metal Pollution Index (HPI) and classifying contamination risk levels.
And show casing contamination level on map based on the location river in your dataset
Problem Statement

Heavy metal contamination in water sources poses serious environmental and health risks. Traditional assessment methods are often time-consuming and lack real-time interpretation.  

This project provides a computational tool to **automate water quality evaluation** using the HPI method, enabling quick and reliable risk assessment.

Live Demo : https://ecogeneers.streamlit.app/

Sample Input & Output

Input:
- Lead (Pb): 0.05 mg/L  
- Cadmium (Cd): 0.01 mg/L  

Output:
- HPI Index: 120  
- Risk Level: High Pollution
